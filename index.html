Biography

Hey there! I’m a Digital Media professional with over 5 years tucked under my belt, weaving through the realms of content creation, digital ad campaigns, and audience engagement across various platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter. Currently calling digital media strategies that align with some pretty important initiatives.

University of Florida saw me rocking the role of a Social Media Coordinator, where I had my hands full curating content, tuning into reproductive issues, and joining creative forces with a talented bunch to roll out coherent campaigns.

Early in the windy city, Chicago, with Jasculca Terman, I cut my teeth in managing client relationships, delving into the digital advertising scene, and optimizing content with Salesforceand Adobe Creative Cloud Suite.

Plus, I’m on the journey of mastering Mass Communication with a side of Web Design and Development at the University of Florida, ensuring every project is ADA compliant.

In a nutshell, I’m all about bridging the gap between art and audience, solving complex puzzles in the digital space, and making connections that count. Whether it’s through compelling content, innovative strategies, or accessible design, I’m here to make a digital mark!

Interests

I'm, a passionate comic book enthusiast with a deep-seated love for the vibrant worlds and dynamic characters that leap off the pages. From the moment I first delved into the colorful panels and gripping narratives, I was hooked! Comics have been a constant companion, offering me an escape into realms of superheroes, villains, and endless possibilities.

I have a particular fondness for the classics – think Spider-Man swinging between skyscrapers and Batman lurking in the shadows of Gotham City. But my love for comics isn't just limited to the caped crusaders; I'm equally drawn to indie comics, where the storytelling is raw, and the art is boundary-pushing. Exploring diverse genres and styles, from fantasy to sci-fi, and drama to humor, has only deepened my appreciation for the medium.

Beyond just a hobby, comics have shaped my worldview, taught me valuable life lessons, and fueled my creativity. I often find myself sketching out my characters and dreaming up my storylines, aspiring to contribute my voice to this dynamic and ever-evolving art form.

In the end, my love for comics is more than just a passion; it's a way of life. Whether I'm flippingthrough the pages of a new release or revisiting a beloved classic, I'm continually discovering, learning, and growing as a person. And I wouldn’t have it any other way!"
